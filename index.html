CITEC Curriculum Overview

Empowering Future Innovators in Technology and Engineering.

The CITEC curriculum is designed to prepare students for successful careers in the rapidly evolving fields of information technology and engineering. Our comprehensive program combines theoretical foundations with practical applications, ensuring graduates are industry-ready and equipped with cutting-edge skills.

Capstone Project Requirements

The Capstone Project is the culmination of your academic journey, where you'll apply everything you've learned to solve real-world problems and demonstrate your expertise in your chosen field.

Minimum Grade Requirement

Students must maintain a grade of 3 or above in prerequisite courses to qualify for the Capstone Project. This ensures you have the necessary foundation to succeed.

Proposal Submission

  • Project proposal with clear objectives
  • Literature review and methodology
  • Timeline and resource planning

Progress Reports

  • Monthly progress updates
  • Mid-semester presentation
  • Advisor consultations

Final Defense & Documentation

  • Complete project documentation
  • Oral defense presentation
  • Working prototype or implementation
